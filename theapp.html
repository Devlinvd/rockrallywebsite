ROCK, RALLY, CONNECT!

You can download the Rock Rally app via the App Store and Play Store. The app is available for free download.

Make an account

Create an account through the steps in the app. We assure you that all your personal information will be safe and kept private.

Personal Preferences

Through the app, you can specify your age range, distance, and gender preferences for swiping

Swipe and Like

By reading descriptions of other Rock Rally users, you can like people and make the first step in finding a new concert buddy.

Make new Connections

You can open the conversation through the chat function. Start with a fun opening line or by sharing your coolest concert experience ever

Match

When both users have liked each other's accounts, a match is created.
